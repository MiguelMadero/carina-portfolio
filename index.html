Patelco Case Study

Length of the project: 2 weeks (school project)
April 2015


In this project I worked with Adrinee Yan, Michael Homnick, Shane Wasley


Client

Patelco is a federally insured, not-for-profit, full-service credit union that offers the same types of products and services as large banks. Like all credit unions, it is based on the principle of cooperation in which people help each other in order to achieve similar financial goals.

Challenge

Patelco has not upgraded their digital offering in years and are seeking a fresh approach that leverages some of the forward thinking technologies/techniques that other upstart financial institutions have been able deliver on.

Problem

Potential Patelco users aren’t getting the information they need to make the switch to Patelco.

Solution

Create an informative homepage that provides the necessary information to guide curious users to contacting Patelco for more information.

Original website does not invite new users to join Patelco.
The new parallax website provides a clear value preposition.

Design Process

Competitor Analysis

We began the project by comparing banks and other credit union websites and compare them to those offered by Patelco.

Competitor Analysis

We went to the closest branch where we interviewed some of the members. Some of the findings during the interview were services they used, areas of improvement for Patelco.

We researched Patelco, who they are, how to become a member, what’s Patelco’s vision, goals and focus in the community.

Survey

We conducted a survey having more than 75 participants were our main goal was to get the follow information

Interviews

From our survey results we interviewed some of the bank users and Credit Union members to enquire more about their needs with their Financial Institution.

Persona

Based on the interviews, surveys and competitively analysis we defined an initial set of personas. After further evaluations we redefined this model and decided to focus exclussively on potential Credit Union members, since they represent the largest area of opportunity.

Problem Statement

Patelco's website is not helping potential members in their decision to join. Also, they don't offer enough information about what is a Credit Union and the advantages they have over banks.

We worked in different sketches bringing each of us a different perspective of our solution.

Iterations

I came up with my first hypothesis with different screens where my persona was able to find information about “What is a CU?”, “Differences with a bank”, “Benefits”, “Easy transition from their bank to CU”, “How Patelco is involved in the community”, “Locations” and “Why to make an appointment with a sales consultant”

The feedback from this session and was very productive, we converged our ideas and we worked individually in a second round of sketches with the decision to make a parallax scrolling website.

In my second hypothesis I have in mind John’s needs I wanted to compiled some of the information from my first sketch and make this more direct making a strong CTA for contacting form.
